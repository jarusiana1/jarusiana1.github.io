UPSCALE SPECS INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS INCORPORATED

Upscale Specs Information and Communications Technology Solutions Incorporated (Upscale Specs ICT Solutions Inc.) is a Philippine-based company established in 2022, specializing in fiber-optic internet, CCTV, and advanced telecommunication services. Headquartered in Dauin, Negros Oriental, it provides innovative ICT solutions aimed at improving connectivity and digital infrastructure in the region.
